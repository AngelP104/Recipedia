Spicy Tuna Sushi Rolls

Submitted by Anthony Lawrence

These Spicy Tuna Sushi Rolls are a delicious and satisfying choice for sushi lovers. The combination of fresh tuna, spicy mayo, and crisp vegetables makes for a perfect bite. Serve with soy sauce and pickled ginger for an authentic sushi experience.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice, cooked and seasoned
  • 1/2 pound fresh sushi-grade tuna, thinly sliced
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup spicy mayo
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • Nori (seaweed) sheets
  • Sesame seeds for garnish
  • Pickled ginger for serving

Instructions

1. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi rolling mat.
2. Wet your hands and spread a thin layer of sushi rice over the nori, leaving a small border at the top.
3. Arrange tuna, cucumber, and avocado slices in the center of the rice.
4. Drizzle spicy mayo over the fillings.
5. Carefully roll the sushi using the bamboo mat, sealing the edge with a bit of water.
6. Slice the roll into bite-sized pieces and sprinkle with sesame seeds.
7. Serve with soy sauce and pickled ginger.
