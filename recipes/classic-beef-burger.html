Classic Beef Burger

Submitted by Peter Davis

Enjoy the classic taste of a perfectly grilled beef burger with this simple and delicious recipe. Whether you're firing up the barbecue or cooking on the stovetop, this classic beef burger is sure to satisfy your burger cravings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef (80% lean)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced red onions
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup and mustard for serving

Instructions

1. Preheat your grill or stovetop pan.
2. In a bowl, gently mix the ground beef with salt and black pepper.
3. Divide the beef into four equal portions and shape them into patties.
4. Grill the burgers for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
5. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
6. Assemble the burgers by placing lettuce, a beef patty, tomatoes, red onions, and pickles on each bun.
7. Serve with ketchup and mustard on the side.
